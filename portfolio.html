About Me

Hi, I'm Sharlene Aniel Dela Cruz, 20 years old from the Philippines. I am a passionate photographer who loves capturing the world through my lens. From landscapes to portraits, I strive to tell a story with each photograph. My website is a reflection of this journey, showcasing not only my best work but also the stories behind the shots. Dive in and discover the beauty as seen through my eyes!

Portfolio

Project 1: Faces of Diversity

Every face on our planet is uniquely captivating, celebrating the beauty found in diverse races and lifestyles. This project highlights the extraordinary individuality present in every person, regardless of their background or way of life.

Project 2: Serenity of the Sea

While it may not replicate the sensation of standing on the shore, this project seeks to evoke the same sense of amazement and ease experienced when immersed in the wondrous sea of Earth.

Project 3: Wildlife Wonders

Animal photography unveils nature in a unique and unseen light. Through the lens, we discover a new perspective, revealing animals from angles that redefine our understanding of the natural world.
